Firms like Wendy’s must account for non-price factors.

  1. Distributional social preferences when comparisons to others are relevant.
  2. Reference points and loss aversion when comparisons to expectations are relevant.

Standard economic models will under-estimate the influence of these preferences on market outcomes.
